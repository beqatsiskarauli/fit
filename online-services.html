plans & pricing

The Standard plan is a great choice no matter your level of fitness. All Forge programs include periodization structure, alternative exercises, creativity and motivation. In addition, you get unlimited text messaging with your personal trainer right through the Forge mobile app. NOTE: This program does not include a custom meal plan. Please request a coupon code from your coach for a discounted custom meal plan.

The Standard plan includes one (1) live video chat or phone call each month to review your next workout plan and answer any questions.

The primary difference between each Forge Online Personal Training program is the frequency of video or phone coaching calls.
